Target Audience

The target audience will be college students and above that are struggling with finances, finding school, or business owners needing assistance with progressing their business.

Persona One:

  • Name: Jeff Daniels
  • Age: 20
  • Title: N/A
  • Employment Status: Unemployeed (but looking)
  • Goals: To recieve a job, and to "Make lots of money"
  • Details: Currently unemployed and not going to college, has desires to enter into college, get a degree, good paying job, and better his future

Persona Two:

  • Name: Bob Romanov
  • Age: 51
  • Title: CEO of Test Industries
  • Employment Status: CEO
  • Goals: To increase personal net worth and work to recieving "passive income"
  • Details: Has worked hard for 20 years to build up his company, even though he is a "small" company, he desires to increase his net worth by increasing his productivity within his company, and learn to make passive income as he wishes to retire in 14 years.

First Senario:

Many young people will want to seek fortune and a "good life". These young seekers will be able to access the website to have a better understand of the world around them and to be able to understand what it takes to become successful as well as come "down to earth" for practical reasoning and ways to gain stable financials.

Second Senario:

The users that have already accomplished their college degree, or are still in college that wish to create their own business to become more self suficent are more than welcome to come to this site and recieve insite on how to prepare, maintain, and finance for a future business. As well as able to brainstorm ideas and learn to collaborate with others to generate a successful business. There will also be classes or workshops to show how to generate passive income with the financials that are already being earned.

Third Senario:

Users that have currently completed their college degree and are currently working, yet seeking a new opportunity will be able to join this site. These users will be able to learn the same as the users in the second senario, as well as how to better finance their lives to provide a better future, and learn to fund themselves in their endevors in a correct way (such as generating passive income).

Fourth Senario

The fourth senario will be small business owners that are just getting started and seeking to grow in their knowledge. These users will be able to learn to better stabalize their finances within their business, how to grow their business, and how to plan ahead to recieve passive income through their personal earnings.

Fifth Senario

This last senario will be for the bigger business owners that wish to generate passive income without the need to work. They already have a stable financial barrier and need more guidence on how to use their income to invest in other businesses or endevors to provide them a passive income for their retirement. These workshops will include information on how to best use their finances and how to look for upcomming opportunities and be able to collaborate with one another to provide the best outcome for their future.
